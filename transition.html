Anthology Ultra Communities to Join
miBUG User Group
The Michigan Blackboard Users Group is a group of faculty, administrators, instructional designers, and technical support staff who use the Blackboard LMS in the state of Michigan and surrounding areas.
Learn Ultra User Group
Led by the community user group leaders for the community. Meets on a regular basis, once every two weeks on a Thursday. The meetings feature presentations, panels, and workshops from institutions and experts.
Teaching in Ultra User Group
This program is an excellent opportunity to discover (or revisit) best practices for using the Learn Ultra classroom to engage students, create content, and more. Explore the Learn Ultra experience from both the learner and educator viewpoint, examining the efficient workflows Learn Ultra, and the Ultra classroom tools provide.
